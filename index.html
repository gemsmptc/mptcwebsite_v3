About

What is MPTC

MPTC stands for “Missional Preparatory Training Course”. It is a Missional Discipleship program which has various expositions mainly for Professionals and Business people.

It is a movement that God inspires the heart of students to understand His mission which has a ripple effect impacting every person they come in touch with, through the way they live (Missional Living) & the work they do (business/ any profession/ homemaking/ studies etc. - Missional Practice)

As the world evolves rapidly in trends such as Globalization and new work culture, the understanding of Missions also evolves... under the sovereign leading of God in such a way that every Christ's Disciple is called to be a Missionary in his/ her own work place to testify about God's Glory by the way they live their life as well by sharing of the Gospel (John 17: 20 and 20: 21).

As modern days' sons of Issachar, we could sense that it's not the evolving trend of Globalization, but it is God who is bringing multi ethnic people into one place i.e in the midst of HIS people to display His Glory in the current world. This we see as God's mission in these last days.
So, at MPTC we equip the Disciple of God to participate in God's Mission based on the below curriculum.

  • Basics of Missional Living/Life style (BML)

  • Profession As Mission (PAM)

  • Business As Mission (BAM)

  • Tent making

  • Ground Reality of SAARC and Indian States

  • Exposure by Senior Missionaries from various mission organization as live Testimonies

  • Exposure by various Mission organizations

  • Various Reformation based Bible Expositions

How

How MPTC Operates?

As MPTC is a Missional Discipleship course, the curriculum is designed to be interactive for the students to actively participate in it. Group discussions and personal pondering questions at every session are designed to make the students think and act accordingly.

  • The course has:

  • 100 hrs. of total sessions

  • Operates after office hours / weekends

  • Runs over a period of 6 months (2 classes per week)

  • 15 days Mission field trip to Bihar, GEMS campus and fields.

  • Any church believer from age 20 to 45 can enroll

  • On request, customized shorter seminars could be made available

  • Interdenominational

"GOD YOU REIGN" - At MPTC, we also offer Taster Seminars called "GOD YOU REIGN" for smaller groups in which the crux of MPTC course is explained, which can then lead the attendees to join a full MPTC course

Who are they?

MPTC is operated by GEMS - Gospel Echoing Mission Society, Bihar. Bro. D. Augustine Jebakumar, General Secretary of GEMS, with the inspiration from God had laid the road map and founded the course. With the God given great burden about today's church, he keeps encouraging many to join/start MPTC to Glorify God's name and build God's kingdom wherever they are placed. He sows the vision about this course wherever God takes him on ministry..

Bro. Isaac Jeyakumar, Sr. Missionary - GEMS, had built the Material for this course in a very effective manner which enables students to see and glorify God. He had also trained Men into leaders who would take up the course into several places and built the Methodology for it to grow as a movement from the founder's road map.

MPTC Resource team comprises of facilitators who operate from Chennai and 17 other different cities, where the course currently runs. They work towards building MPTC to new heights by establishing MPTC @ Every State & Every SAARC country The team of facilitators have a great burden from God about His church and on the believers understanding of God and His mission. With this, they contagiously work with churches, prayer groups and individuals to pass on the right understanding about God and His mission. The team works between GEMS and churches in a model that breaks Secular - Sacred divide i.e they do their secular job as mission for full time as well as work in sacred world doing it as mission for full time. By the grace of God, all of the MPTC teams operating out of 17 different cities are running in the same model.
